About BCS

BCS is a three-year-long undergraduate course that deals with the key aspects of computer science and its applications in several real-life scenarios. The course imparts knowledge on programming fundamentals and hardware fundamentals, which are quite similar to the B.Tech course. The course provides major insights into basic programming and operations along with some basic electronics and controllers that form the hardware part of the computer.

About M.Sc. Computer Science

M.Sc. in Computer Science is a two-year post-graduate programme which has Curriculum under Choice Based Credit & Grading System. With the objective to develop human resources with core competence in various thrust areas of Computer Science. The programme includes software engineering, system development, natural computation, mathematical foundations and artificial intelligence. Other modules include programming, data analytics, software development, applied communications, network architecture, and database design.
